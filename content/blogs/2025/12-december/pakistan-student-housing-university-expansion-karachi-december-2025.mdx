Parents of university students face a problem: their children need furnished, secure apartments near campus, not investment properties. They want 1-2 bedroom units with shared study spaces, gym access, and security. ## The Student Housing Segment

Student housing inquiries surged 89% year-over-year. Unlike family apartments, student units optimize for different features: proximity to universities, shared common areas, security, and flexible lease terms (10-month cycles matching academic calendars).

Graana data shows 62% of student housing inquiries come from parents seeking furnished units near FAST, IBA, and Iqra University. Rental yields are lower (6-8% vs. 10-12% family apartments), but occupancy rates exceed 94%—students aren't speculation-prone.
