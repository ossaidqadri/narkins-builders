## What is Location Liquidity?

Location liquidity refers to how easily and quickly a property can be sold at its fair market value. Properties in prime, in-demand locations with proven appreciation tend to have high liquidity. Properties in remote, underdeveloped, or less desirable areas have low liquidity â€“ they sit on the market for months, often requiring significant price reductions.
